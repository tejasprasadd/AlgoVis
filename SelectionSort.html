Selection Sort Visualization

Insights

Selection Sort is a simple sorting algorithm that repeatedly finds the minimum element from the unsorted part and moves it to the end of the sorted part.

  • Best-case time complexity: O(n²)
  • Average-case time complexity: O(n²)
  • Worst-case time complexity: O(n²)
  • Space Complexity: O(1)
  • Best For: Small datasets and scenarios where memory writes are costly
  • Not Suitable For: Large datasets due to poor performance
